About Us

On this page you can find writing by members of the Re-enchantment collective. This is a chill, low stakes place to share works in progress, experiments, or pieces you just want to get out in the world. We welcome hobbyist writers, professionals, writers exploring new forms, guys who just wrote one poem in their notes app and wanted to show it to someone. There's no selectivity or editorial process here, anything you send in will get posted. It's just a space for the community to share their work!
